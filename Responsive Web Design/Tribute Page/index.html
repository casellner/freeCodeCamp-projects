Jean Craighead George

A tribute page of my favorite author

Inspiration

"I just love to write and I love to write about animals, and the children found me. I wasn't sure what I'd written.... My first books were animal biographies about a mink and racooon and I kept getting letters from children. I thought, "there's my audience, I love animals and they love animals.""

- Jean Craighead George in an interview with the New York Puiblic Library

Notable works

  • My Side of the Mountain
  • Julie of the Wolves (won the 1973 Newberry Medal)
